## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my data stored on my device?**
Yes. SteadyDose stores all data locally on your device. No account is required and no data is sent to external servers.

**How do I export a session report?**
PDF and CSV export are available on the Session Complete screen and on any history entry. Export requires a SteadyDose Pro subscription.

**How do I restore a completed session?**
Open the History tab, find the session, and tap the restore icon or the "Track Again" button to start a new session with the same settings.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions can be managed through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone.

**The app isn't available in my language — can you add it?**
SteadyDose currently supports English, French, Spanish, and Portuguese. Send us an email if you'd like to request another language.

## Privacy Policy

SteadyDose does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All session data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.
